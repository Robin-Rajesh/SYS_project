# EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK: MISSION & BENEFITS
# ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. MISSION STATEMENT
- "To empower global commerce through data-driven precision and sustainable innovation."

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
- Employees are eligible for the 'Flexible Workspace' program (3 days remote, 2 days in-office).
- A one-time $500 'Home Office Stipend' is provided to all new hires.

3. SALES COMMISSION STRUCTURE
- Base Commission: 5% of Net Sales.
- Accelerator Level 1: If 110% of quota is met, commission increases to 7.5% for all sales.
- President's Club: Top 5 performers annually receive an all-expenses-paid trip to Maui.

4. HEALTH & WELLNESS
- Premium health insurance coverage begins on Day 1 of employment.
- Annual $300 "Wellness Credit" for gym memberships or mental health apps.

5. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
- The company reimburses up to $2,500/year for job-related certifications or courses.
- Internal "Data Fellowship" program allows non-technical staff to learn SQL and Python.
