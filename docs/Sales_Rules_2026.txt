# SALES COMPLIANCE & DISCOUNT RULES (Q1 2026)
# ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. STANDARD DISCOUNT LIMITS
- All sales associates are authorized to provide a standard discount of up to 15% without prior approval.
- High-volume corporate client status grants an automatic 10% discount across all categories.

2. REGIONAL CAPS
- West Region: Maximum discount is strictly capped at 25% due to high shipping overhead.
- South Region: Promotional "Summer Blitz" allows for up to 30% discounts on Furniture only.
- Central/East Regions: Follow standard 15% rules.

3. APPROVAL HIERARCHY
- Discounts between 16% and 30%: Requires written approval from the Regional Sales Manager.
- Discounts exceeding 30%: Requires sign-off from the VP of Global Sales.

4. BUNDLING RULES
- Customers purchasing 'Technology' and 'Office Supplies' together receive an additional 5% 'Workspace Bundle' discount.
- Bundling discounts cannot be combined with seasonal promotional codes exceeding 20%.

5. AUDIT TRIGGER
- Any transaction where the discount exceeds 40% will trigger an automatic internal compliance audit.
